Create and collaborate with TypeDraw!

TypeDraw is a cross-platform app designed for and by creative minds to enable users to collaborate with others while they create works of art. Open it on your desktop browser or download the app and create on the go!

How do I use TypeDraw?

  • Create a Document

    Select "Create a New Doc" to get an empty document started or visit the public document to see what other are up to.

  • Type and Draw

    Select "Type" and then click on the page to start typing. Select "Draw" then click and drag around the screen to draw a picture.

Why did we make TypeDraw?

We made TypeDraw as a way to develop and display crucial industry skills such as mobile-first development, experience in the MERN stack and React Native, as well as working as a team of developers through Git.

We hope to continue developing TypeDraw moving forward and aspire to publish it onto the App Store, Google Play Store, as well as full-browser compatibility.
