Create and collaborate with TypeDraw!

TypeDraw is a cross-platform app designed for and by creative minds to enable collaboration between users while they create works of art. Open it on your desktop browser or download the app and create on the go!

How do I use TypeDraw?

  • Create a Document

    Select "Create a New Doc" to get an empty document started, or visit the public document to see what others are up to.

  • Type and Draw

    Select "Type" and then click on the page to start typing. Select "Draw" then click and drag around the screen to draw a picture.

Why did we make TypeDraw?

We made TypeDraw as a way to develop and display crucial industry skills such as mobile-first development, MERN stack and React Native experience, and team collaboration through Git.

We hope to continue developing TypeDraw moving forward, and aspire to publish it on the App Store and Google Play Store, as well as maintain cross-browser compatibility.
